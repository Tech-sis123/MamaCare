Key Problems Addressed By This MVP
For The Doctor
1. Being unsure of how many patients
He will get to interact with in a day
2. Not being able to know which patients
to see first in order of priority
For The Patient
1. Long waiting hours to book for
Antenatal and on each clinic day
before seeing their doctor
2. They have questions about pregnancy
and sometimes get to settle for
random information online
3. Inability to tell which symptom is
severe and which symptom is normal
during pregnancy
This will be solved by scheduling of patients
before Clinic time as they will be given
specific time slots to meet with their Doctor.
This saves him a lot of time and makes him
more efficient in his work
High risk and low risk patients will be
Identified before hand and communicated to
the doctor, so he knows that patients to give
priority to at
They get to book from the comfort of their
homes and have the opportunity to know the
exact time they will meet with their doctors
without waiting in line for several hours
They get weekly health education and what to
expect at each stage of their pregnancy and
each information is from a doctor’s
perspective, not just random information
online.
The tool keeps record of all their symptoms
and monitors symptoms that point towards
hypertension and pre-eclampsia.
It raises an alarm when an abnormality is
noticed. This helps patients get early
treatment and in turn prevent severe issues
that can arise from late treatment
